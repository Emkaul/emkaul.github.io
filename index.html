What is USSD?

USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) sometimes referred to as "Quick Codes" or "Feature codes" is a Global System for Mobile(GSM) communication technology that is used to send text between a mobile phone and an application program in the network. Applications may include prepaid roaming or mobile chatting.

Highly Innovative USSD Service

USSD is similar to Short Messaging Service (SMS), but,unlike SMS, USSD transactions occur during the session only. With SMS, message scan be sent to a mobile phone and stored for several days if the phone is not activated or within range.
